About Me

Hello! My name is Muhammad Talha Khan. I am a dedicated and detail-oriented Software Engineer with a strong foundation in developing efficient and scalable web and mobile applications. I am passionate about solving complex problems and continuously enhancing my technical skills.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of South Asia, graduating with a CGPA of 3.44. During my final year, I led the mobile development of our capstone project titled "Hifazat" — a community-driven safety awareness application aimed at enhancing public safety through real-time crime reporting and analytics.

The app allows users to report incidents by submitting location-based data, time, crime type, and a description. It also features live updates, crime heatmaps, voting mechanisms on authenticity, and visual crime statistics (daily, weekly, and monthly). The project was built using Flutter for the mobile frontend, integrated with a Django REST API backend and PostgreSQL/PostGIS for geospatial data handling. The app empowers communities by promoting transparency and collective awareness.

My core expertise includes technologies such as React, Node.js, and Python, along with practical experience in RESTful APIs, and building end-to-end solutions. I take pride in writing clean, maintainable code and thrive in collaborative, fast-paced environments.
